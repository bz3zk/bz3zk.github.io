## First time writing here.

This is the first post I'll write using the [Chirpy](https://github.com/cotes2020/jekyll-theme-chirpy) theme.

I started a blog about a year ago, but I didn't have the discipline or dedication to keep it going. Sometimes I didn't even know what to write about, so it eventually ended up abandoned in a corner (just like a hundred other projects throughout my life).

But this time it's different. "I swear bro, this is the one bro, just one more blog bro."

I want to write about the things I'm learning, maybe some small projects I'm building, and why not, even personal stuff like what I'm reading or watching (which counts as personal stuff in my eyes). I also want to improve my writing because English isn't my first language (you can probably tell). Even though I speak it fluently, writing in English is still challenging for me.

If you're reading this right now, go do something else. I'm just getting started. (But please come back in a month or so when I hopefully have something real to offer.)
